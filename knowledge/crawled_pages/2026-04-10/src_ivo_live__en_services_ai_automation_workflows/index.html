FAQ

Does this replace our current tools?

No. The focus is integration into your current stack with clear handover logic.

How is workflow safety handled?

With explicit access paths, review stages, and strict separation between assistance and final approval.

When do we see first effects?

With a focused scope, measurable impact is often visible within the first 2 weeks.
