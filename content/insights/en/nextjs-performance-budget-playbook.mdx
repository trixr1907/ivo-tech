Performance should be treated as a product constraint, not a late audit.

## Budget fields

- LCP (lab): &lt;= 2.0s
- CLS: &lt;= 0.05
- JS on critical route: &lt;= 170KB gzip

## Guardrails

Every PR touching core routes gets Lighthouse checks and a budget review.
